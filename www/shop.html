Uses

Use Liyanga as a deep conditioner.

Product Details

Liyanga is a deep conditioner made from food based ingredients. Liyanga works with your natural hair texture, imparting softness, moisture, lustre and shine, all while simplifying your wash-day. All you need is your favorite shampoo and Liyanga to obtain soft, tangle-free, healthy and manageable hair.

Directions

  1. Wash your hair with your favorite shampoo.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. To moist and damp hair, apply Liyanga generously from hair root to hair shaft. We prefer to apply Liyanga in sections, braiding each section after application.
  4. Place hair cap over hair to which Liyanga has been applied.
  5. Leave Liyanga on for at least 1 hour (longer if you desire).
  6. After the hour, rinse Liyanga from hair and style as needed.
